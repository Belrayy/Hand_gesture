About What We Do & Who We Are

We’re two passionate engineering students building Prometheus as our end-of-year project.
Prometheus is more than just a project, it’s our vision of intuitive, touchless control in action.

Fixing Issues About

We take every issue seriously. Whether it’s a bug, a glitch, or a suggestion, we actively monitor and resolve problems to ensure Prometheus stays reliable and stable.
